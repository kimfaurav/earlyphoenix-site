Key Findings & Recommendations

Key Findings

  • Market size: 8,607 active listings across 5 platforms, with 137 species identified across 6 categories
  • Welfare data gap: Unlike cats/dogs, reptile platforms offer virtually no structured welfare fields. Only 11.6% of listings contain any welfare keyword in their description
  • Description analysis: Welfare keywords like "captive bred" and "health check" appear in free-text descriptions, making text analysis essential for welfare intelligence
  • Pricing: Median price £150, with significant variation by species (range £5 – £49,995)
  • Platform landscape: MorphMarket dominates as the specialist marketplace, with Preloved, Pets4Homes, and FreeAds providing general classifieds coverage

Recommendations

  • Engage platforms to add structured welfare fields for reptile listings (captive bred, health checked, CITES documentation)
  • Develop NLP analysis of description text to extract welfare signals from unstructured content at scale
  • CITES monitoring: Build automated CITES compliance checking using species identification and Article 10 certificate mentions
  • High-volume seller monitoring: Track sellers with 10+ active listings for potential unlicensed breeding operations
  • Longitudinal tracking: Establish monthly scraping cadence across all 5 platforms to track market trends over time
